    • Hi, I'm Naia!

    I'm a Border Collie with a blue merle coat. Everyone confuses me with an Australian Shepherd, but it's understandable. I have traits more aligned with the Border Collie breed. I love hearding anything I can, running around, playing, and especially staring.

    My story begins in 2013. I was born, then left to fend for myself in the woods. Lucky for me, someone found me! Despite her best efforts, she was unable to find my original owners.

    Wet, and scared!

    Let's face it, though; it was for the best. I was wet, scared, and covered in ticks. She took me home, removed the ticks, and gave me a bath. I had bitten through my tongue at some point in my adventures alone, but I wasn't any worse for wear. Plus, now I have a cute chip in it! However, this would be the start of our wonderful journey together.

    I've grown a lot since I was rescued. I live happily, and healthily with my Mom, and her partner. We love playing monkey in the middle with my favorite toys, going to the park, and taking strolls together.

    I love them very much, and they love me.

    It's my Mommy!
